## Full Description (4000 characters max)

PingWeather sends you a push notification before bad weather hits — not after. You set the conditions, we watch the forecast, you get a ping when it matters.

Built for people who work and live outdoors:

LIVESTOCK OWNERS
Set a freeze alert for your ranch. When the forecast drops below your threshold at your location, you get a notification hours in advance — enough time to break ice, move animals, or prep water heaters. No more checking the forecast every morning.

HUNTERS
Track cold fronts, wind shifts, and precipitation windows at your hunting spots. Set compound conditions like "temperature drops below 40 AND wind under 10 mph" to know exactly when the deer will move. Works across multiple locations — your stand, your lease, your food plot.

OUTDOOR WORKERS
Rain delay risk? High wind safety concern? Set alerts for the job site and get notified before conditions go sideways. One less thing to worry about on a busy morning.

HOW IT WORKS
1. Add a location — search by name or drop a pin.
2. Set your conditions — temperature, precipitation chance, wind speed, humidity. Single conditions or compound (AND/OR) logic.
3. Choose your polling interval — how often we check the forecast against your rules.
4. Go about your day. We handle the rest.

PingWeather checks forecasts server-side on your schedule. When conditions match, you get a push notification. No background battery drain. No constant location tracking. Your rules run on our servers, not your phone.

FEATURES
- Temperature, precipitation, wind, humidity, and weather code alerts
- Compound conditions with AND/OR logic (Pro and Premium)
- Multiple monitored locations (up to 10 on Premium)
- Polling intervals from 1 hour to 12 hours depending on tier
- 14-day forecast view with hourly drill-down
- Preset templates for common scenarios (freeze warning, rain alert, high wind)
- Alert history so you can review past notifications
- Dark mode and multiple themes
- No ads. No data selling. No tracking beyond what the service needs.

FREE TIER
1 location, 2 alert rules, 12-hour polling, 7-day history. Enough to try it out with one critical location.

PRO — $3.99/month
3 locations, 5 rules, 4-hour polling, 30-day history, compound conditions.

PREMIUM — $7.99/month
10 locations, unlimited rules, 1-hour polling, 90-day history, compound conditions, SMS alerts (coming soon).

IMPORTANT: Weather forecasts are inherently uncertain. PingWeather supplements official weather warnings — it does not replace the National Weather Service or emergency alerts. Always maintain backup alert channels for life-safety decisions.
